About the Intelligence Anomaly Office (IAO)

The Intelligence Anomaly Office (IAO) is dedicated to the systematic interpretation of anomalies across multiple domains: maritime, aviation, satellite, geological, environmental, historical, behavioural, and missing‑person patterns.

IAO exists to provide clarity in uncertain environments. We focus on signals that traditional analysis overlooks—outliers, irregularities, and weak indicators that, when properly understood, reveal risk, opportunity, and hidden structure.

IAO is being developed as a disciplined, privacy‑respecting, intelligence‑grade organisation. Our aim is to serve investigators, analysts, and organisations who require precision, discretion, and reliability in the way anomalies are identified, contextualised, and communicated.

IAO is currently in its early access and development phase. As our capabilities grow, we will introduce dashboards, digests, and tools designed to make anomaly intelligence accessible without noise or theatrics.
